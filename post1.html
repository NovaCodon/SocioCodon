Why were the names Severe Techno and Neera Yahlium Retired?

Published: 20/10/2025 12:09 PM IST
Last edited: 23/04/2026 08:27 PM IST
@NiralBhatt

Why we discontinued Severe Techno

First of all Happy Diwali to everyone! This is our first ever post on SocioCodon. Continue reading:

  • The accounts on wiki platforms that were created under the name "Severe Techno" were easy to hack due to weak passwords, and this led to mild vandalism across many wiki platforms.
  • Multiple accounts used the same name and icon, making unnecessary edits.
  • These accounts were blocked by those platforms for long periods.
  • My professional Instagram account, "Severe Techno" was hacked due to a weak password, raising concerns about possible misuse.
  • To avoid these issues, I decided to stop Severe Techno and continue under a new name. And thus I started experimenting under a new name, "Neera Yahlium".

Why we silently stopped using the name Neera Yahlium

  • The name "Neera Yahlium" was a meaningless, AI-generated name created in a hurry, because of the previous issue. It was difficult to remember, didn’t sound very tech-oriented, and actually felt quite boring. Many people suggested me to adopt a more meaningful and concise name instead, and thus this decision was necessary.
  • Another reason for the same was that I was experimenting with different ideas under this name during an initial learning and prototyping phase, with an emphasis on legacy. This was the main reason for a quiet rebrand.
  • As the direction matured, it became clear that emphasizing timelines or legacy was unnecessary. Thus, the project, under Niral Studios, was therefore quietly rebranded as "NeuroYott" to better reflect its actual scope and vision, and to adopt a more tech-oriented, meaningful, and concise identity.
  • Subsequent branding changes were made to unify identity and maintain consistency across platforms. The current name represents the refined and official direction of the work.
