Why Severe Techno and Neera Yahlium Were Retired and Gaslighted?

Posted on: Oct 20, 2025 | 12:09 PM
@NiralBhatt

Why we discontinued Severe Techno

First of all Happy Diwali to everyone! This is our first ever post on SocioCodon

  • Severe Techno faced vandalism on many wiki platforms.
  • Multiple accounts used the same name and icon, making unnecessary edits.
  • These accounts were blocked by those platforms for long periods.
  • My Instagram account was hacked, raising concerns about potential misuse.
  • To avoid these issues, I decided to stop Severe Techno and continue under a new name.

Why we “Gaslighted” the name Neera Yahlium

  • I initially created the Neera Yahlium IDE in Python 2.7, presenting it as if it had existed since 2011.
  • The goal was to make people believe the business was older than it actually was.
  • Later, I realized lying about the origin was unnecessary.
  • I quietly removed all references to Neera Yahlium and renamed it to NeuroYott, suggesting Severe Techno was directly renamed to NeuroYott.
  • Eventually, I decided to use a name I had previously used in 2023 for a new channel: NovaCodon.
  • On Sep 1, 2025, NovaCodon was rebranded to align with the original branding and eliminate the old short form “NY.”
