LORD Trick to Remember Oxygenated & Deoxygenated Blood: Class 10

Published: 05/01/2026 8:07 PM IST
@NiralBhatt

Many Class 10 students get confused while remembering which side of the human heart contains oxygenated blood and which side contains deoxygenated blood.

Here is a very simple and exam-friendly trick to remember it forever. This trick is called the LORD Trick.

LORD Trick

LO → Left side Oxygenated
RD → Right side Deoxygenated

Just remember the word LORD, and you will never mix it up in exams.

Explanation

  • The left side of the heart (left atrium and left ventricle) contains oxygenated blood.
  • The right side of the heart (right atrium and right ventricle) contains deoxygenated blood.

Oxygenated blood is pumped from the left ventricle to the whole body, while deoxygenated blood returns to the right side of the heart from the body.

This concept is very important for Class 10 Biology and frequently appears in board exams as MCQs and short-answer questions.

Why this trick works

  • Easy to remember
  • No confusion in exams
  • Perfect for quick revision

So whenever you feel confused, just recall one word: LORD.
